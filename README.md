🚀 TaxMind AI
📌 Overview

TaxMind AI is an intelligent AI-powered legal and tax research platform designed to simplify tax analysis, legal assistance, and financial research through advanced AI capabilities. Built with a modern and responsive interface, the platform delivers a fast, secure, and user-friendly experience for students, professionals, and researchers.

The project combines smart AI interactions with a clean digital experience to help users explore complex tax and legal concepts more efficiently.

✨ Key Features

✔️ AI-powered tax & legal research assistance
✔️ Modern and responsive user interface
✔️ Smart query handling and research workflow
✔️ Interactive dashboard-inspired design
✔️ Fast and optimized performance
✔️ Scalable frontend architecture
✔️ Real-time AI interaction system
